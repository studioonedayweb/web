About Lúcás

More than
fixing things

The name Ó Dathaire is rooted in old Irish — the ideas of colour, light, and restoration. It is not just a trade name. It is a way of working.

For over 15 years, Lúcás has worked across Dublin providing reliable painting, wall repairs, door repairs, and general handyman services. No complications. No rushed work.

Good work is about restoring warmth, balance, and life back into a space.
