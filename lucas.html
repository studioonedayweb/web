About

The name Ó Dathaire is rooted in old Irish — ideas of colour, light, and restoration. Not just a trade name. A way of working.

For over 15 years Lúcás has worked across Dublin: reliable painting, wall and door repairs, plastering, general handyman. No complications. No rushed work.

Good work is about restoring warmth, balance, and life back into a space.
