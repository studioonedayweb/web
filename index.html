How it works

to live page, in one day.

You see the live page. We refine. Your feedback shapes the final version. No guesswork, no surprises.

Page goes online. Files delivered. Your digital presence starts working — and it belongs to you, forever.

I
Discovery call
We start with conversation. Who are your clients? What do you want them to do when they find you? What feeling should your page give? Everything I learn here becomes the foundation of the page.
II
Design & build
I write the code, design the layout, choose the colours and typography, craft the words. For Dublin clients I can visit your space and photograph it. Everything happens in one focused session.
III
Delivery & handover
You receive the live page and all source files. Pure HTML — no lock-in, no platform, no monthly fees. Host it anywhere. Move it freely. It is yours.
