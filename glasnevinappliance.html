About

North Dublin’s trusted appliance service.

Glasnevin Appliance Services provides clients across North Dublin with professional home appliance repairs, servicing and spare parts. With over 17 years of professional experience, the business has become a local repair point for Dublin 9 and the surrounding area.

The team can repair a wide range of home, garden and kitchen appliances. Smaller appliances can be dropped into the shop directly, while larger appliances such as washing machines and dishwashers can be handled through a home visit service.

The aim is simple: repair where repair makes sense, source the right part where possible, and keep practical household equipment working for longer.
